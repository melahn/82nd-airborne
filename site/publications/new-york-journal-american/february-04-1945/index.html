February 4, 1945

Overview

The New York Journal-American was a daily newspaper published in New York City from 1937 to 1966.

It included a Pictorial Review section that contained news from the war front and a mix of local, humor and show business news.

This copy came from Joseph M Melahn who, that week, lost his son, Joseph L Melahn, in the fighting in Europe. He kept this copy until the day he died in 1965.

This copy was scanned by his grandson Gregory Melahn.
