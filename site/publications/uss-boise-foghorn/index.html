USS Boise Foghorn - Christmas Edition, 1945

Overview

The USS Boise was a light Cruiser that saw action in World War 2. After the end of the war the ship particpated in Operation Magic Carpet, the transportation of US military personnel from their overseas assignments back home to the United States. The crew published a newsletter, The Foghorn. This is the 12-25-1945 edition of The Foghorn.

This document was created from a copy left by George C. Melahn who served in the 505'th Parachute Infantry in WW 2 and who was transported home to Queens, NY, from Europe on the USS Boise It was digitally scanned by his son Gregory Melahn.
