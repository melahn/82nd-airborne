NYPE News - Welcome Home Edition

Overview

The New York Port of Embarkation (NYPE) was a U.S. Army Command responsible for the transportation of military personnel to their overseas locations. In 1945, it served as a point of welcome for returning U.S. service personnel coming home from overseas.

In September of 1945, the Office of Technical Information of the NYPE issued a 'Welcome Home' Special Edition of the NYPE News explaining to the returning troops all the services they would find at the NYPE and how they would make it to the next point in their trip home by rail or plane.

This document was created from a copy left by George C. Melahn who served in the 505'th Parachute Infantry in WW 2. It was digitally scanned by his son Gregory Melahn.

The document has also been uploaded to the Internet Archive
