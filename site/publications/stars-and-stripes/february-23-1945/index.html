February 23, 1945

Overview

The Stars and Stripes is a daily newspaper published for U.S. military personnel.

These documents were created from copies left by George C. Melahn who served during World War 2 in the 505'th Parachute Infantry Regiment of the 82nd Airborne Division. They were scanned by his son Gregory Melahn.
