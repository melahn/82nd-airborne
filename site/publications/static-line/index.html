505'th Parachute Infantry Regiment Static Line

The Static Line was a publication of the 505'th Parachute Infantry Regiment (PIR) in World War 2.

These documents were created from copies left by George C. Melahn who served in the 505'th PIR. They were scanned by his son Gregory Melahn.

Issues

  • November, 1944
  • January, 1945 "Edition Belgique"
  • November, 1945 "The Final Berlin Edition"
