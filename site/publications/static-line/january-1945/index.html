January, 1945

Overview

The Static Line was a publication of the 505'th Parachute Infantry Regiment (PIR) in World War 2. The January, 1945 edition was published in Belgium during the Battle of the Bulge.

This document was created from a copy left by George C. Melahn who served in the 505'th PIR. They were scanned by his son Gregory Melahn.
