Memorial Service

On Thursday, May 29, 1947, an 82nd Airborne Memorial Service was held in the Oak Room of the Hotel Capitol located at 8th Avenue & West 51st Street in New York City. The guest speaker was none other than Major General James M. Gavin, former division commander.

Here are the pages from the program for that event. These pages were from a copy left by George C. Melahn who served in the 505'th Parachute Infantry in WW 2. They were digitally scanned by his son Gregory Melahn.
